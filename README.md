  <h1 align="center"> Culturize </h1>
<p align="center">A <b>Google DSC Solution Challenge</b> submission</p>

<p align="center">Culturize is a unique app that's on a mission to revive endangered cultures. It's an exciting platform that brings together people from different corners of the world to share their cultural stories, traditions, and practices. With Culturize, you can explore and learn about different cultures, connect with like-minded individuals, and contribute to preserving our rich and diverse heritage. Get ready to embark on a fascinating journey of discovery with Culturize!</p>

## 📝 Project Info
The contents in this project follow the following structure, where all views are containerised into screens.

```
├───models
|
├───screens
│   ├───auth
│   ├───learn
│   ├───blogs/content
│   ├───explore
│   ├───community
│       ├───chat
│   ├───profile
```

## ℹ️ About

<p> Built with flutter and firebase Culturize celebrates and preserves cultural traditions worldwide through blog posts(audio and text), workshops, and educational programs. Our project provides a platform for artists and artisans to showcase their talents, connect with like-minded people, and educate the public about the significance of these practices in shaping the identity and heritage of different communities. </p>

### Here is the quick tour on how our app inspire people to take action to preserve cultural heritage for future generations.

<div align="center"><img src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/97936126/228750753-88443163-8ea3-4b48-bcbb-0da79980270a.png" width="200" height="400" ></div>
After installing our app, Users can either log in with their email or sign up within the app.

#### <h3><ins>Overview on learning section.</ins></h3>
After completing the process, you will be directed to the home page, which is a learning section. It contains blogs in text and audio formats, allowing users to learn about cultures they are interested in.

#### <h3><ins>Overview on search section.</ins></h3>
On the search section, users can search for specific tutorials, communities to join, and workshops to attend relevant to their interests. We integrated Google Maps so that the top results displayed are within the user's geographic range, unless they specify a location.

#### <h3><ins>Overview on community section.</ins></h3>
If you are a first-time user, you will be directed to the Top Communities page, where all the active communities are displayed. From there, you can join communities, interact with fellow enthusiasts, and register for workshops they conduct. The communities you have joined will be displayed in your Communities section.

If there are new blogs that you have favorited or any events that are updated in your communities, you will receive notifications so you don't miss out on any updates or opportunities.
